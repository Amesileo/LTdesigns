TYPOGRAPHY.

A selection of Lettering Art and typographic experiments that are a result of pushing my own technical boundaries and skill.

THE LOFT.

Inspired by London’s club music scene, I re-branded a beloved queer nightclub in Norwich as a reminder of how significant these spaces are to the queer community.

A TALE OF CRUMBS.

This family-friendly exhibition takes you on the journey of biscuits and how they have earned its rightful place as a staple British delicacy.

A fun and playful approach to art direction and typography creates an exhibiton that fills you up with fun, bite-sized knowledge. It also leaves you feeling peckish.

THIS IS ME.

BASICALLY...

I’m a graphic designer with a keen interest in typography and quirky, off-beat branding. Since graduating university, I have worked within various aspects of the design industry and have been experimenting with different formats. Whether I am marketing a beloved local theatre or creating logos for office workspaces, I prefer a personality-focused approach to design that can open up a whole plethora of ideas.
